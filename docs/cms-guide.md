# Admin CMS Guide — Punjab Laptop Sirsa

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Go to `https://your-site.com/admin/login`
2. Enter your credentials:
   - **Email:** admin@punjablaptopsirsa.com
   - **Password:** (your password)
3. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

---

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard shows:
- **Total Products** — Number of laptops listed
- **In Stock** — Products currently available
- **Unread Inquiries** — New customer messages
- **Active Offers** — Currently running deals

---

## Managing Products

### Add a New Product

1. Go to **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Product**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Dell Inspiron 15 3520"
   - **Brand** — Select from dropdown
   - **Category** — Gaming, Office, Student, etc.
   - **Processor** — e.g., "Intel Core i5-1235U (12th Gen)"
   - **RAM** — e.g., "8 GB DDR4"
   - **Storage** — e.g., "512 GB SSD"
   - **Display** — e.g., "15.6\" FHD (1920x1080)"
   - **Graphics** — e.g., "Intel UHD Graphics"
   - **Price** — Original price in ₹
   - **Sale Price** — Discounted price (leave empty if no discount)
   - **Images** — Upload product photos
   - **Description** — Detailed product description
   - **Warranty** — e.g., "1 Year Dell Warranty"
   - **Condition** — New, Like New, Excellent, Good, Refurbished
   - **Featured** — Toggle to show on homepage
   - **In Stock** — Toggle availability
4. Click **Save**

### Edit a Product

1. Click the **Edit** button on any product row
2. Modify any fields
3. Click **Update**

### Delete a Product

1. Click the **Delete** button
2. Confirm the deletion
3. ⚠️ This action cannot be undone

### Mark Sold / Out of Stock

1. Click **Edit** on the product
2. Toggle **In Stock** off
3. Click **Update**

---

## Managing Offers

### Create an Offer

1. Go to **Offers**
2. Click **Add Offer**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — e.g., "Summer Gaming Sale 🔥"
   - **Description** — Offer details
   - **Discount Type** — Percentage, Flat, or Special
   - **Discount Value** — e.g., 10 (for 10% or ₹10,000)
   - **Valid From / Until** — Date range
   - **Banner Image** — Upload an eye-catching image
   - **Active** — Toggle to show/hide
4. Click **Save**

---

## Managing Blog Posts

### Write a Blog Post

1. Go to **Blog**
2. Click **Add Post**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — SEO-friendly title
   - **Content** — Write your article (HTML supported)
   - **Excerpt** — Short summary for listing page
   - **Cover Image** — Upload header image
   - **Tags** — Add relevant tags (e.g., "gaming", "guide")
   - **Published** — Toggle to publish/draft
4. Click **Save**

### Blog Writing Tips for SEO

- Include keywords: "laptop Sirsa", "gaming laptop", "student laptop"
- Write 500+ words per post
- Use headings (H2, H3) to structure content
- Include your shop name and address
- Link to product pages when mentioning laptops

---

## Managing Banners

### Add a Homepage Banner

1. Go to **Banners**
2. Click **Add Banner**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — Banner heading text
   - **Subtitle** — Supporting text
   - **Image** — Upload banner image (recommended: 1400×600px)
   - **Link** — Page to navigate to when clicked
   - **Sort Order** — Lower number = appears first
   - **Active** — Toggle visibility
4. Click **Save**

---

## Viewing Inquiries

1. Go to **Inquiries**
2. View all customer contact form submissions
3. Click on any inquiry to see full details
4. Click **Mark as Read** to track handled inquiries
5. Reply to customers via WhatsApp or email

---

## Image Upload Tips

- **Product Photos:** Use clear, well-lit photos on white/neutral background
- **Recommended Size:** 800×800px minimum for product images
- **Banners:** 1400×600px for optimal display
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG (JPG recommended for smaller file size)
- **File Size:** Keep under 2MB for fast loading
